Practice Answers

  • Random moves: collect non-empty pits and use random.choice(); seeded for reproducibility.
  • State tree: implicit on recursion stack; children are clones via clone_game().
  • Best move: try each legal move, clone/apply, evaluate recursively, pick highest backed-up utility.
  • Utility: original = mancala diff; v2 adds 0.5× side-stone diff to reward mid-game strength.
  • Why deeper plies: see captures/sweeps; cost grows ~branching^depth (≈6^d).
  • Minimax vs Alpha-Beta: same decisions for fixed depth; alpha-beta prunes and runs much faster.
